ALL YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED...

Why blood and saliva?

  • Blood and saliva is what makes us human, and more importantly it holds has all the answers to how we learn about our bodies.
  •  
  • But the problem right now is no one really understands it? We’re working on translating this data in an easier way so that you can understand, access and measure your health over time.
Why do I need a membership to Base?

  • Our Base membership gives you unlimited access to our digital app, which is the home for everything you need to learn about tracking your health goals.
  •  
  • Think of Base as your new support system for optimizing your health on the go. Our membership covers the fees for our medical team that are ordering and reviewing the tests for you. Additionally, it covers your personalized results analysis and monthly diet plans in our digital app.
  •  
  • The membership also gives you access to other perks like free shipping, affordable testing, unlimited messaging with the science team and more.

Do I have to do a finger prick?

  • No, we also have saliva tests available like the starter tests for Stress and Sleep. If you don't feel comfortable doing the test yourself you can also walk-in to one of our partner labs center.
Is my data safe?

  • We ensure complete secure privacy and data usage, our engineering team makes sure that all the data that travels through our systems about you is encrypted and we don't provide this to any third parties.
  •  
  • For more information visit our Privacy Policy.
How much does it cost?

  • If you select Base Pro, you should have all you need to measure, learn and improve about a track included for $59.95/mo
  •  
  • If you select Base Lite, you should get everything you need for meaningful insights, learn what's your % of optimization over time for a given track for an average of $19/mo

  • For more info check our how it works section.
