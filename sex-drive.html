How can I track sex drive with a saliva test?

A saliva test is a widely used medical tactic to measure vitamin, hormone, and neurotrasmitter levels. Measuring your saliva will allow Base to determine which supplements, behavioral changes, and routines will help optimize for your goals.

A saliva test will provide the exact Testosterone and Cortisol measurement found in your body. From here, you'll be able to identify how your levels measure up to our identified health ranges based on your body's makeup.

What steps can I take after I take a Base SexDrive Test?

Your results will come in 3-4 days after submitting your sample. You'll be given updates on your test status and when you're results have been uploaded. After viewing your results, users can begin tracking challenges, designed to further optimize your vitamin and hormone levels. Additionally, our doctors will evalutate a suggested area to track based on your previous results.

Who can I reach out to if I have questions about testing and my results?

You can ask any questions to your Base physician that reviewed your results or to our Science team via in-app messaging. Additionally, Customer Support is always here to help answer your questions.
