How do we test the strength of your immune system?

A strong immune system is the key to overall wellbeing and health. Through our simple blood test we are able to measure micronutrients that suppor the Immune system: your Zinc, Magnesium, Copper and Heavy Metals. Additionally, we measure Immunoglobulins (antibodies) levels. These antibodies in your blood fight off bacterias, viruses, and toxins.

What am I testing?

Antibodies are your main tool for recognizing and notifying your body’s defense system to fight illness. When you have that data in-hand, our team will work with you to optimize your immunity through looking at micronutrients that support your immune system and your antibodies.
