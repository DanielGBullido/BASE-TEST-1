ALL YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED...

Can I track multiple things?

  • Yes, you can select more than one track at a discounted rate. During checkout select your additional tracks for an added $55 per track.
  •  
  • You can change tracks at any point during your membership. We will check in with you regularly to learn about new symptoms or struggles to properly adjust your testing plan for your included tests.
  •  
  • You can also manage what and when to track from your Base app.

Are these tests covered by HSA/FSA?

  • Yup, in most cases, you can use your HSA/FSA account to pay for Base tests/membership! Your Base tests are authorized by a physician, so it should fit the guidelines for most HSA/FSA coverage.
  •  
  • To apply your FSA/HSA, you'll purchase the test by paying out of pocket, or with your FSA/HSA card if you have one directly at checkout. Shoot us a message at info@get-base.com to get a special receipt and send it to your plan, make sure they are submitting it as a wellness test!
  •  
  • If you are unsure if your plan will cover it, we encourage you to check in with them prior to subscribing or purchasing a test.

Why do I need a digital membership to Base?

  • Our Base membership gives you unlimited access to our digital app, which is the home for everything you need to learn about tracking your health goals.
  •  
  • Think of Base as your new support system for optimizing your health on the go. Our membership covers the fees for our medical team that are ordering and reviewing the tests for you. Additionally, it covers your personalized results analysis and monthly diet plans in our digital app.
  •  
  • The membership also gives you access to other perks like free shipping, affordable testing, unlimited messaging with the science team and more.

When can I cancel?

You can cancel or downgrade at anytime and you’ll still get to keep all of your data accessible under your account.

What if I want to pause my subscription?

A base member will always reach out to you before your next test is shipped.

You can decide to skip the next delivery, pause your plan or change frequency based off your needs and goals at any point.
