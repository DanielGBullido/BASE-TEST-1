Collection method

For your first test we will use an easy finger-prick test kit. This is the primary collection method for this track.

What's being measured?

Key hormone, protein, and vitamin levels in your body that directly affect your metabolism. Get direct insight into potential nutrient deficiencies or food sensitivities you might experience from your diet. Start understanding how different foods impact your lifestyle with data directly from your body’s response with the diet diagnostic panel.

Your Diet tests

Over time, your testing plan will evolve to fit your goals and the way your body responds to the changes you make or the challenges you take. Over time we will build out your complete diet diagnostics panel one test at a time and continue to monitor and optimize.

This test will look at your general metabolic status. We measure HDL, LDL, Total Cholesterol and Triglycerides. HDL: This is known as your “good cholesterol” and it works towards reducing the amount of blockage in your arteries promoting blood flow. LDL: typically known as the “bad cholesterol” and will determine the blockage to your arteries and will hinder blood flow. Triglycerides: This is the fat stored in your body that is released as energy between your eating cycles. Total Cholesterol: used as a benchmark to measure heart health.
