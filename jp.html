WHAT MAKES UP
MY LIPIDS PANEL?
Your lipids panel is made up of your LDL (bad cholesterol), HDL (good cholesterol), Total Cholesterol, and Triglyceride levels.
HOW WILL THIS
HELP ME?

Getting your lipids within a healthy range and keeping them that way will not only help to balance the hormones that impact your metabolic weight, it will also help facilitate a healthier diet. Bonus- healthy lipid levels = higher energy + better overall health!
