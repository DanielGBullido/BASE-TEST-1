Collection method

For your first test we will use an easy finger-prick test kit. This is the primary collection method for this track.

What's being measured?

Key hormone and vitamin levels associated with your energy and optimal brain function. We will look into building out a clear diagnostic panel around levels affecting key memory and concentration functionality, your ability to learn and focus, and limiting physical and mental fatigue.

Your Energy tests

Over time, your testing plan will evolve to fit your goals and the way your body responds to the changes you make or the challenges you take. Over time we will build out your complete energy diagnostics panel one test at a time and continue to monitor and optimize.
