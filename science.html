How do we calculate our scores?

  • We use data science, mathematics and predictive analysis to look at how your biomarkers impact your overall wellness.
  • After testing your blood or saliva we calculate a track score based on your biomarkers.
Where do our recommendations come from?

  • Our Base Science team is in charge of tailoring algorithms based on research, proven clinical trials and studies.
  • Your Track score is analyzed based on your biomarker analysis.
  • Our doctors and nutritionists then make lifestyle, diet and supplement recommendations to improve your score.

Why blood?
Blood, the world’s most tailored outfit, it has all the answers to how we learn about our bodies. You don’t need an entire vial of blood, we can get all the information we need from a finger prick.
01.FINGER PRICK
Why finger prick?

We care about getting the most effective results in the least invasive way. A finger prick test is quick, painless and non invasive. We can use the finger prick for both dried blood spot cards and microtainers.

What can it tell us?

We can measure some biomarkers that are present in blood by capturing very little amount of blood. This method is widely used in pediatrics.

02.SALIVA
Why saliva?

We use our saliva tests for measuring sleep and stress hormones. Saliva is where hormones get stuck and it’s easy to test. Saliva is the mediator of the bodies physical condition and a great indicator for when something is off track.

What can it tell us?

The salivary glands show us where chronic pain is by indicating different hormone levels such as cortisol, enzymes and antibodies.
